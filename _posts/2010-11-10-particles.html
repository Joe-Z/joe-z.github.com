A little project which can produce different-looking and -behaving particles.

Every particle is influenced by outside-factors such as gravity and wind and bounces of the ground (or not) accordingly to its bounciness.

Furthermore I made a 'tornado-emitter' whose particles fly in a tornadlo-like manner.
