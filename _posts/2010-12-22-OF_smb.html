Well, the title pretty much says it. This is the first level of Super Mario Bros. programmed with openFrameworks.

The game provides a 2-player mode, where 2 players can run through the level and whoever reaches the end first, wins.

There are also goombas running around, just waiting to be flattened!

more

This project is actually composed of several smaller projects I and my fellow student Benjamin Medicke made for our game development course (asset-manager, scrolling backgrounds, spritesheet-animation, map-editor).

The tile-/images were taken from an image found at ladyada.net.
