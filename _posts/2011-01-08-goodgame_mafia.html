From August 2011 to January 2012 I interned at Goodgame Studios in Hamburg, Germany. I worked on the backend-side of the browser game Goodgame Mafia (now called Goodgame Gangster). My responsibilities included:

  • Developing and updating the server components
  • working closely with (flash) client developers and game design
  • maintaining and extending the game database (MySQL)
