Agar Agar puts you in the role of a scientist observing and controlling colonies in a petri dish.

This was my first WebGL project. I really enjoyed the experience and learned a lot. If you're interested, you can give it a try at agaragargame.com

Or just try the simplified demo to the left!

It lets you control one unit of the game (the 'particle'). Try the swipe-controls (click & drag). If the particles are close to each other they will flock together.
